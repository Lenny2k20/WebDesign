Pokémon Anime History

Pokémon is the brainchild of Satoshi Tajiri. Born in Tokyo in 1965, Pictured above is the main protagonist Ash Ketchump from Pallet Town in the Jhoto region. The word Pokémon is the contracted form of “Pocket Monsters”, or “Poketto Monsuta” in its original Japanese. It is represented in many types of media, most famously video games, a still-running anime series, manga, and a trading card game.

There are over 896 pokemon with more and more being added each growing year. These 896 Pokemon are split up into different generations. There are currently 7 different generations of pokemon.
